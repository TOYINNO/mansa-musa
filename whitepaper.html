Whitepaper

Mansa Musa, the 14th-century emperor of the Mali Empire, is renowned for his immense wealth and generosity. His pilgrimage to Mecca in 1324, accompanied by a lavish display of gold and riches, left a lasting impression on the world. Today, $MANSA seeks to channel the spirit of Mansa Musa, bringing back his legacy of wealth and abundance to the modern age.

Just as Mansa Musa's wealth transformed the regions he traveled through, $MANSA is poised to bring disruptive and transformative wealth into the crypto ecosystem. Tokenomics

The tokenomics of $MANSA are designed to ensure fair distribution, robust marketing, team incentives, and smooth exchange listing. Below is the breakdown of the $MANSA token allocation:

  • Total Supply: 100,000,000 $MANSA
  • Airdrop: 95,000,000 $MANSA (95%) to Andrew Tate
  • Marketing: 2,000,000 $MANSA (2%)
  • Team: 2,000,000 $MANSA (2%)
  • Exchange Listing: 1,000,000 $MANSA (1%)

Airdrop

A significant portion of the $MANSA supply, 95%, will be airdropped to Andrew Tate. This strategic airdrop aims to leverage his influence and reach to bring widespread attention and adoption to $MANSA. By associating with a prominent figure, we anticipate rapid growth in our community and market presence.

Marketing

To ensure sustained growth and visibility, 2% of the total supply is allocated for marketing efforts. This will cover various initiatives, including social media campaigns, influencer partnerships, and promotional events, to spread awareness and attract new users to the $MANSA ecosystem.

Team

Recognizing the importance of a motivated and dedicated team, 2% of the total supply is reserved for the core team. These tokens will serve as incentives to ensure the team's commitment to the project's long-term success and continuous development.

Exchange Listing

To facilitate seamless trading and liquidity, 1% of the total supply is allocated for exchange listing. This will cover listing fees and related expenses to make $MANSA accessible on major cryptocurrency exchanges.
